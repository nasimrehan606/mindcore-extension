# Purpose

This document defines the official logo system for the MindCore ecosystem.

It establishes how the logo should be designed, displayed, scaled, and protected across all platforms to ensure consistency and brand recognition.

These guidelines apply to the Browser Extension, Website, Mobile App, Desktop App, Social Media, Documentation, Presentations, and Marketing Materials.

---

# Logo Philosophy

The MindCore logo represents the connection between human intelligence and artificial intelligence.

The name is divided into two meaningful parts:

**Mind**
Represents learning, curiosity, creativity, critical thinking, and knowledge.

**Core**
Represents the central intelligence that powers learning, symbolizing AI, innovation, and technology.

Together, the logo reflects the idea that intelligent technology strengthens human learning rather than replacing it.

# Logo Clear Space

The minimum clear space around the logo should equal the height of the letter "M".

No text, icons, or graphics should enter this space.

# Incorrect Usage

Never:

✗ Stretch the logo.

✗ Compress the logo.

✗ Rotate the logo.

✗ Change the typography.

✗ Add shadows.

✗ Add outlines.

✗ Change the official colors.

✗ Place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.

✗ Rearrange the order of "Mind" and "Core".

# Design Decisions

## D-010

Decision

The official logo is a wordmark with "Mind" in black and "Core" in gold.

Reason

The color split reinforces the two-part meaning of the brand.

Impact

All official logos must preserve this distinction.

Status

Accepted

Date

2026-06-29

---

## D-011

Decision

The official icon is a simplified "M".

Reason

Provides strong recognition at small sizes.

Impact

Used for the Chrome Extension, mobile app, favicon, and profile icons.

Status

Accepted

Date

2026-06-29

---

## D-012

Decision

The logo should remain minimal and avoid unnecessary visual effects.

Reason

A clean design improves recognition and longevity.

Impact

Animations and decorative effects should always remain subtle.

Status

Accepted

Date

2026-06-29
