# Project Overview

MindCore is an AI-powered learning ecosystem designed to help students, professionals, and lifelong learners study more effectively.

The platform combines artificial intelligence with modern educational tools to simplify learning, improve understanding, and make knowledge more accessible.

MindCore is not just a single application. It is a connected ecosystem consisting of a browser extension, website, mobile application, desktop application, and AI assistant that work together to support learning.

---

# Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted AI-powered learning platforms by making education intelligent, accessible, and personalized for everyone.

---

# Mission

To empower learners through AI by providing tools that improve understanding, save time, and encourage continuous learning.

---

# Core Objectives

- Build an AI-powered learning ecosystem.
- Create a seamless learning experience across all platforms.
- Encourage understanding instead of memorization.
- Design intuitive and accessible interfaces.
- Protect user privacy and data.
- Deliver reliable and trustworthy AI assistance.

# Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when:

- Users can upload documents easily.
- AI generates accurate summaries.
- AI creates useful quizzes.
- Users enjoy a consistent experience across products.
- Rabi becomes a trusted learning assistant.
- The platform maintains strong performance and security.

# Design Decisions

## D-030

**Decision**

MindCore will be developed as a complete learning ecosystem rather than a single application.

**Reason**

A unified ecosystem provides a better learning experience and allows users to access their tools across multiple platforms.

**Status**

Accepted

**Date**

2026-06-30

---

## D-031

**Decision**

Education and learning outcomes take priority over adding unnecessary features.

**Reason**

MindCore's mission is to help users learn effectively, not to compete on feature quantity.

**Status**

Accepted

**Date**

2026-06-30
