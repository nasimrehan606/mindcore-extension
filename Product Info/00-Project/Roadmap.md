# Design Decisions

## D-034

**Decision**

MindCore will follow a phased development approach.

**Reason**

Breaking development into structured phases improves planning, reduces risk, and allows gradual expansion.

**Status**

Accepted

**Date**

2026-06-30

## D-035

**Decision**

The Browser Extension and Website are the first public products.

**Reason**

These platforms provide the fastest path to delivering value while establishing the MindCore ecosystem.

**Status**

Accepted

**Date**
