# Purpose

The Decision Log is the official record of significant decisions made during the planning, design, development, testing, and maintenance of MindCore.

Its purpose is to:

- Record why decisions were made.
- Preserve project knowledge.
- Reduce repeated discussions.
- Improve transparency.
- Help future contributors understand the project's history.

Every important decision should receive a unique Decision ID (D-XXX) and be documented here.

---

# Decision Status

Each decision must have one of the following statuses:

- Draft
- Proposed
- Accepted
- Rejected
- Deprecated
- Superseded

# Decision Guidelines

A new Decision ID should be created whenever:

- A project direction changes.
- A technology is selected.
- A design principle is adopted.
- A feature is approved or rejected.
- A security policy is introduced.
- An architectural change is made.

Minor wording or formatting updates do not require a new Decision ID.

---

# Numbering Convention

Use sequential numbering.

Examples:

- D-036
- D-037
- D-038

Decision IDs are never reused.
