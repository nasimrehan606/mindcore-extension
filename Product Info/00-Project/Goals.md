# Purpose

This document defines the strategic goals of MindCore.

These goals provide a clear direction for development and help ensure that every feature contributes to the project's mission and long-term vision.

---

# Mission Goal

Build an AI-powered learning ecosystem that helps learners understand, retain, and apply knowledge more effectively through intelligent and accessible tools.

---

# Vision Goal

Become one of the world's leading AI learning platforms by delivering reliable, user-friendly, and innovative educational experiences.

---

# Strategic Goals

## G-001

### Build a Unified Learning Ecosystem

Create a connected platform where the Browser Extension, Website, Mobile App, Desktop App, and Rabi AI work together seamlessly.

**Priority:** Critical

**Status:** Planned

---

## G-002

### Deliver High-Quality AI Learning Tools

Develop AI features that genuinely improve learning, including:

- AI Document Reader
- AI Document Summarizer
- AI Quiz Generator
- AI Flashcards
- AI Chat with Rabi

**Priority:** Critical

**Status:** Planned

---

## G-003

### Create an Exceptional User Experience

Design interfaces that are:

- Clean
- Fast
- Accessible
- Responsive
- Easy to understand

**Priority:** High

**Status:** Planned

---

## G-004

### Build Trust Through Transparency

Users should always understand:

- What the AI is doing
- Why it generated a response
- What data is being used
- How their information is protected

**Priority:** High

**Status:** Planned

---

## G-005

### Support Continuous Learning

Encourage learners to improve over time by providing:

- Progress Tracking
- Learning Streaks
- Quiz History
- Flashcards
- Study Planner

**Priority:** Medium

**Status:** Planned

# AI Goals

Rabi should become:

- A Tutor
- A Learning Coach
- A Research Assistant
- A Writing Assistant
- A Study Companion

Rabi should focus on helping users understand concepts rather than simply providing answers.

# Long-Term Goals

- Support multiple languages.
- Expand AI capabilities.
- Integrate with educational platforms.
- Introduce collaborative learning features.
- Build an ecosystem recognized for quality and innovation.

# Guiding Principle

Every new feature should answer one question:

> **"Does this help people learn better?"**

If the answer is no, reconsider the feature.
